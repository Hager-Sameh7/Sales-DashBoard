# 📊 Sales Analysis Dashboard (Excel)

---

## **📌 Overview**
An **interactive Excel dashboard** transforming raw sales data into a comprehensive view of business performance, profitability, and customer distribution. It enables data-driven decision-making through actionable insights.

---

## **🎯 Key Objectives**
* Analyze overall **sales and profit performance**.
* Identify top and bottom-performing **products**.
* Visualize geographic **customer distribution**.
* Evaluate the impact of **discounts on profitability**.

## **📊 Dashdoard Features**
* **🔹 KPI Cards:** Quick overview of critical metrics.
* **🔹 Visual Charts:** Profit & sales breakdown by category, sub-category, and products.
* **🔹 Geographical Map:** Customer distribution across U.S. states.
* **🔹 Interactive Filters (Slicers):** Filter by Year, Quarter, and Category.

---

## **💡 Key Insights**
* **Technology category** generates the highest profit.
* **California, Texas, and New York** are top-performing states.
* High discounts in certain areas are significantly reducing overall profit margins.

## **🚀 How to Use**
